Summary of the study results

1. Capomulin and Ramicane have the biggest number of mice per treatment. 
Propriva has a lower number of mice per treatment. 
2. For the study was used more male mice than females.
3. Data showed that Capomulin and Ramicane are more efficient than Infubinol and Ceftamine.
4. The correlation between mouse weight and the average tumour volume is 0.84.
What suggests a strong correlation that the average volume of the tumour increase 
and decrease depending on the weight.
